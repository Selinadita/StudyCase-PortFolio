ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Jaehyun! I was born Jeong Yoon-oh on February 14, 1997 in Seoul, South Korea. I am a singer, actor, and member of the boy band NCT under SM Entertainment. I was fortunate to be a part of the sub-units NCT U and NCT 127, where I was able to express myself through music and dance.

NCT Mambers

SM Entertainment (2016-Now)

My debut with NCT in 2016 was one of the most rewarding moments of my life. Since then, I have participated in various group and solo projects, including hosting and acting in television dramas. These experiences have given me many valuable lessons and the opportunity to meet many amazing people. Achievements With NCT, we have won various awards, such as:
1. Mnet Asian Music Awards: Best New Artist (2016)
2. Seoul Music Awards: Bonsang Award (2019, 2020)
3. Golden Disc Awards: Bonsang Album Division (2020)
Every award we receive is a testament to the hard work and overwhelming support of our fans.
